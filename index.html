STEP 1: Choose Your Program
Our initial assessment form is designed to help you determine the right program suited for your body type as well as your goals
STEP 2: Brainstorm With Your Buddy
Once you are enrolled, your Buddy will contact to learn about your goals and design The 5-Point Program that will get you there
STEP 3: Join Our Community
Your Buddy will guide and support you through your journey, but there are thousands of people on our growing online community here.
STEP 4: Break Through Plateaus
Are there any sticking points in your progress? Talk to your Buddy to optimize your plan for ongoing results.
STEP 5: Celebrate Your Milestones
No win is too small – be it higher energy levels throughout the day, clearer skin, defined muscle s, or a drop in your weight.
